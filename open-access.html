Open Access, Copyright & Fees

Home > Policies > Open Access & Fees

Open Access Statement

Lex Humanitas is a fully open-access academic journal. All articles published by the journal are made immediately and permanently available online, free of charge to any reader, without subscription fees or registration barriers.

The journal subscribes to the Budapest Open Access Initiative (BOAI) definition of open access. This means that users are permitted to read, download, copy, distribute, print, search, or link to the full texts of articles, crawl them for indexing, pass them as data to software, or use them for any other lawful purpose, without financial, legal, or technical barriers other than those inseparable from gaining access to the internet itself.

Copyright & Licensing

Authors publishing in Lex Humanitas retain copyright in their work.

All articles are licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International Licence (CC BY 4.0). Under this licence, readers are free to:

  • Share — copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format.
  • Adapt — remix, transform, and build upon the material for any purpose, including commercially.

These freedoms are granted provided that appropriate attribution is given to the original authors and Lex Humanitas, a link to the licence is provided, and any changes made are clearly indicated.

Author Rights & Self-Archiving

Authors retain the right to self-archive their work. They are encouraged to deposit all versions of their article (Pre-print, Author Accepted Manuscript, and Publisher's Version/PDF) in personal blogs, institutional repositories, or subject-specific repositories (such as SSRN, Academia.edu, or ResearchGate) at any stage, provided that proper bibliographic citation is given to Lex Humanitas as the original venue of publication.

Publication Fees (APC)

To support academic dissemination and reduce financial barriers for authors, particularly in the Global South, the inaugural issue, Volume 1, Issue 1 (2026), is published entirely free of any Article Processing Charge (APC).

There is no charge to authors for manuscript submission, double-blind peer review, editorial processing, typesetting, or open-access publication of this inaugural issue.

Fee Policy Notice: Any change to this fee position for subsequent issues will be announced on this page and on the homepage well in advance. Authors who have already had their papers accepted under the prevailing no-fee policy will not be charged retrospectively under any circumstance.
