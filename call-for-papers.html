Current Invitation — Volume 1, Issue 2 (2026)

Manuscripts are invited for the upcoming issue of Lex Humanitas: Journal of Law, Society and Humanities. Researchers, academicians, legal professionals, and policy experts are encouraged to submit original research articles, review papers, case comments, and book reviews reflecting interdisciplinary scholarship.

Publication Frequency & Submission Deadlines

Lex Humanitas publishes articles **quarterly**, with issues released in **March, June, September, and December** each year.

To ensure timely review and publication, the editorial desk operates on a rolling submission basis. Authors intending to submit their manuscripts for a particular quarterly issue should note the deadlines below:

Quarter 1 Issue

March Release

Deadline: 30 March

Manuscripts received after March 30 will be evaluated for the June cycle.

Quarter 2 Issue

June Release

Deadline: 30 June

Manuscripts received after June 30 will be evaluated for the September cycle.

Quarter 3 Issue

September Release

Deadline: 30 September

Manuscripts received after September 30 will be evaluated for the December cycle.

Quarter 4 Issue

December Release

Deadline: 30 December

Manuscripts received after December 30 will be evaluated for the subsequent year's March cycle.

*Note: Manuscripts received after the stipulated deadline will ordinarily be considered for the subsequent issue, subject to the editorial team's discretion and reviewer availability.
