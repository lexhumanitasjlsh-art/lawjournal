Inaugural Issue Volume 1, Issue 1 (2026) Released

The editorial desk is pleased to announce that the inaugural issue, Volume 1, Issue 1 (2026), is now available online as a fully open-access publication. All papers in this inaugural issue have been published entirely free of any Article Processing Charge (APC).

We express our sincere gratitude to all contributing authors, peer reviewers, and advisory board members whose scholarly dedication and academic support made this launch milestone possible.
